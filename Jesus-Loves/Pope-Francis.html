Jesus Loves Pope Francis

About Pope Francis

Pope Francis, born Jorge Mario Bergoglio, is the 266th Pope of the Roman Catholic Church. He is known for his humility, emphasis on God's mercy, and commitment to social justice. His papacy has been marked by a focus on caring for the poor and marginalized, as well as promoting interfaith dialogue.

Scripture on Love and Mercy

"For God so loved the world that he gave his only Son, so that everyone who believes in him might not perish but might have eternal life."

— John 3:16 (USCCB)

"But God, who is rich in mercy, because of the great love he had for us, even when we were dead in our transgressions, brought us to life with Christ (by grace you have been saved), raised us up with him, and seated us with him in the heavens in Christ Jesus, that in the ages to come he might show the immeasurable riches of his grace in his kindness to us in Christ Jesus."

— Ephesians 2:4-7 (USCCB)

Jesus' Love for All

Jesus Christ, the Son of God, loves all people unconditionally. His teachings emphasize love, forgiveness, and compassion. Pope Francis, as the Vicar of Christ, continues to spread this message of love and mercy to the world.
