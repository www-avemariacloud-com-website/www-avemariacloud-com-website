Pope Francis: A Modern Shepherd

Pope Francis, born Jorge Mario Bergoglio, has been the Bishop of Rome and the leader of the worldwide Catholic Church since March 13, 2013. His papacy has been marked by a focus on compassion, mercy, and care for the marginalized.

His teachings emphasize the importance of living the Gospel message in our daily lives and caring for our common home, the Earth. Pope Francis has been a strong advocate for social justice and has worked to reform various aspects of the Church.

The Message of Love

The core of Jesus' teaching is love - love for God and love for neighbor. Pope Francis has been a powerful voice in reminding us of this fundamental truth. His papacy has been marked by a renewed focus on the corporal and spiritual works of mercy.

From his famous phrase "Who am I to judge?" to his extensive writings on environmental stewardship in Laudato Si, Pope Francis has challenged us to live out our faith in practical ways that demonstrate God's love for the world.
