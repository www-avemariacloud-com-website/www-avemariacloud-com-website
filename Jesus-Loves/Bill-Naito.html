Who Was Bill Naito?

Bill Naito was a prominent businessman and community leader in Portland, Oregon, known for his commitment to social justice and his Christian faith. His life exemplified the principles of compassion, integrity, and service to others.

Bill Naito's Christian faith was central to his life and work. He actively supported various charitable organizations and community initiatives, demonstrating his commitment to living out his faith through service to others.
