Jesus Loves The Jewish People

Welcome to this web page that explores the love Jesus Christ has for the Jewish people. There is a deep connection between Christianity and the Jewish people.

The Jewish Roots of Christianity

Jesus Himself was Jewish and He fulfilled the prophecies of the Old Testament. The Jewish people hold a special place in God's plan of salvation.

Scripture regarding God's Love for Israel

"For the gifts and the call of God are irrevocable."

Romans 11:29 (USCCB)

"But you, Israel, my servant,
Jacob, whom I have chosen,
offspring of Abraham my friend—
You whom I have taken from the ends of the earth
and summoned from its far-off places,
To whom I have said, You are my servant;
I chose you, I have not rejected you—"

Isaiah 41:8-9 (USCCB)

Prayer for Unity

Let us pray for the unity of all Christians, that we may come to a deeper understanding of God's love and His plan for humanity.

"That they may all be one, as you, Father, are in me and I in you, that they also may be in us, that the world may believe that you sent me."

John 17:21 (USCCB)
