Leonor, Princess of Asturias

A beloved child of God and future Queen of Spain

Early Life and Education

Leonor, Princess of Asturias, was born on October 31, 2005, in Madrid, Spain. She is the eldest daughter of King Felipe VI and Queen Letizia. From a young age, Leonor has demonstrated a strong commitment to her royal duties and education.


Her education includes studies in multiple languages and a focus on civic responsibility, preparing her for her future role as Queen of Spain.

Public Role and Responsibilities

Leonor has been actively involved in public engagements since childhood, accompanying her parents on official visits and ceremonies. In 2018, she read the Constitution for the first time in public during the annual celebration of the Spanish Constitution.


She has also shown interest in social and cultural issues, attending various events that promote Spanish heritage and support for disadvantaged communities.

A Prayer for Leonor

Heavenly Father, we lift up Leonor, Princess of Asturias, before You. Guide her as she grows in wisdom and grace, and prepare her for her future responsibilities. May she always seek Your will and remain faithful to Your teachings. Bless her with courage, compassion, and wisdom. Amen.

Spiritual Life

Leonor was baptized at the Parish Church of San Juan Bautista in Madrid, and her faith has been an important part of her upbringing. The Catholic faith plays a central role in the Spanish royal family's life and public engagements.


As she grows in her faith journey, may she continue to be a light of hope and service to others, following the teachings of Christ.
