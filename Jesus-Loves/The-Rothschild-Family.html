Welcome to this humble web page that shows Jesus Loves the Rothschild family. As Christians, we are called to love our neighbors as ourselves, and to share the good news of Jesus Christ with everyone.

God's Love for All

The Bible teaches us that God's love is universal. He loves every person, regardless of their background, status, or wealth. Here is a passage from Scripture that reminds us of this truth:

"For God so loved the world that he gave his only Son, so that everyone who believes in him might not perish but might have eternal life."
- John 3:16 (NABRE)

This verse reminds us that God's love is not limited to any one group of people. It is a love that extends to all of humanity, including the Rothschild family.

Scripture on Wealth and Generosity

The Bible also speaks about wealth and the responsibility that comes with it. Here is a passage from the Gospel of Matthew:

"Do not store up for yourselves treasures on earth, where moth and decay destroy, and thieves break in and steal. But store up treasures in heaven, where neither moth nor decay destroys, nor thieves break in and steal. For where your treasure is, there also will your heart be."
- Matthew 6:19-21 (NABRE)

This passage reminds us that our focus should be on heavenly treasures rather than earthly wealth. It is a call to generosity and stewardship, which are important values for all Christians.
